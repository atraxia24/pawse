Why We Exist?

Every animal deserves love, safety, and care. Pawse was created to rescue stray and injured animals, provide medical attention, and help them find caring forever homes. By connecting helpless paws with compassionate humans, we aim to give every animal a second chance to thrive.

How It Works?

1. Report an Animal

See a stray or injured pet? Submit a quick report through our platform.

2. NGO Notification

Our system connects your report to the nearest animal rescue NGO.

3. Treatment & Update

The NGO treats the animal, and you get notified once the animal has received care.
